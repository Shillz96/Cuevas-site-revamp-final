Our Heritage

Crafting authentic western wear since 1978

The Cuevas Legacy

Founded in 1978 by Manuel Cuevas, a master craftsman with deep roots in traditional western bootmaking, our family-owned business began with a small workshop in San Antonio, Texas. Manuel's vision was simple yet profound: create authentic western footwear that honored the traditions of the American West while embracing innovation and exceptional quality.

For over four decades, we've stayed true to our founding principles, combining time-honored techniques with modern comfort and durability. Each pair of Cuevas boots is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using only the finest leathers and materials sourced from trusted suppliers who share our commitment to quality.

1978

The Beginning

Manuel Cuevas opens his first small workshop in San Antonio, Texas, specializing in handcrafted western boots.

1985

Expanding the Craft

The workshop grows into a full store, adding western apparel and accessories to the Cuevas collection.

1997

Second Generation

Manuel's children join the business, bringing fresh ideas while maintaining the family's commitment to quality.

2010

Digital Frontier

Cuevas Western Wear launches online, bringing authentic western tradition to customers worldwide.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

Now with multiple locations and a thriving online presence, we remain dedicated to the values that started our journey.
